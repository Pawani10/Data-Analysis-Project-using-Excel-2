# Bike Buyers Customer Analysis
## Project Objective
To analyze customer demographic and behavioral data to identify the characteristics of individuals who are most likely to purchase a bike — enabling data-driven decisions for marketing targeting, product positioning, and sales outreach.
# Dataset used
<a href="https://github.com/Pawani10/Data-Analysis-Project-using-Excel-2/blob/main/Excel%20Project%20Dataset%20(1).xlsx"> View dataset </a>

## KPIs Tracked
- Bike Purchase Rate by Gender
- Average Income of Buyers vs. Non-Buyers (by Gender)
- Purchase Rate by Commute Distance
- Purchase Rate by Age Group (Adult / Middle Age / Old)
- Purchase Rate by Region (Europe, Pacific, North America)
- Purchase Rate by Occupation and Education Level
